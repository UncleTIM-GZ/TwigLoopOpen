# Privacy Requests

**Effective date:** 2026-03-16
**Last updated:** 2026-03-16
**Service:** Twig Loop (twigloop.tech)

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to request:

- **Access:** A copy of the personal data I hold about you.
- **Correction:** Updates to inaccurate personal data.
- **Deletion:** Removal of your account and associated personal data.

---

## How to Submit a Request

Send an email to **privacy@twigloop.tech** with the following information:

1. **Your registered email address** (the one you used to sign up for Twig Loop).
2. **What you are requesting:** access, correction, or deletion.
3. **Details:** For correction requests, specify what data is inaccurate and what it should be changed to. For access requests, let me know if you want all data or specific categories.

---

## Response Time

I will respond to your request within **30 calendar days** of receiving it.

If your request is unusually complex or I receive a high volume of requests, I may need up to an additional 30 days. If so, I will notify you of the delay and the reason within the initial 30-day period.

---

## Identity Verification

To protect your data, I may need to verify your identity before processing your request. Verification will typically be done by confirming access to your registered email address (e.g., by sending a verification link or code to that address).

I will never ask for sensitive documents like a passport or ID card for privacy requests.

---

## What Happens When You Request Deletion

When you request account deletion:

1. Your account will be deactivated immediately.
2. Your personal data (email, display name, bio) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
3. Content you created (projects, task cards) will be deleted or disassociated from your identity.
4. Some anonymized, aggregated data may remain (e.g., total project counts, platform-wide statistics). This data cannot be linked back to you.
5. Backup copies will be purged within 7 days as part of the rolling backup cycle.

---

## Limitations

- I can only process requests related to data held by Twig Loop. For data held by third-party services (GitHub, Cloudflare), you will need to contact those providers directly.
- Deletion of your account is permanent and cannot be reversed.
