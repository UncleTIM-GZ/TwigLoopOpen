## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as whether you are logged in.

---

## Cookies We Use

During the Public Beta, Twig Loop uses **essential cookies only**. These are strictly necessary for the service to function.

| Cookie | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|--------|---------|------|----------|
| Session cookie | Maintains your login session so you don't have to sign in on every page | Essential | Expires when you log out or after inactivity |
| Auth token cookie | Stores your authentication token securely (httpOnly, secure flags) | Essential | Access token: 30 minutes; Refresh token: 7 days |

---

## Cloudflare Cookies

Twig Loop uses Cloudflare for CDN and DNS services. Cloudflare may set cookies on your device for security purposes:

| Cookie | Purpose | Set by |
|--------|---------|--------|
| `__cf_bm` | Bot detection and management | Cloudflare |
| `cf_clearance` | Records that you passed a security challenge | Cloudflare |

These cookies are set by Cloudflare, not by Twig Loop directly. You can read more about Cloudflare's cookies in [Cloudflare's cookie policy](https://developers.cloudflare.com/fundamentals/reference/policies-compliances/cloudflare-cookies/).

---

## What We Do Not Use

- No advertising or tracking cookies.
- No third-party analytics cookies.
- No social media tracking pixels.
- No cross-site tracking of any kind.

## Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Here are links to instructions for common browsers:

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/manage-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using Twig Loop.
